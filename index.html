About

BRET is a research initiative that explores how renewable energy development can be aligned with biodiversity conservation. We provide datasets, tools, and case studies to support biodiversity-friendly energy transitions in Europe and beyond.

Project data and analysis scripts are openly available on GitHub. Larger datasets are archived in open repositories with DOIs.
