Stakeholder Survey: Biodiversity & Renewable Energy

The BRET project invites stakeholders to share their perspectives on the conflicts and synergies between renewable energy expansion (solar, wind, hydropower, bioenergy) and biodiversity protection.

Research Information & Consent

This survey is conducted as part of the BRET project (Biodiversity-positive Renewable Energy Transition, 2026–2029). The purpose is to understand stakeholder perspectives on renewable energy expansion and biodiversity conservation.

Participation is voluntary. Responses are anonymous. No personal identifying information is collected. Data will be used for academic research, policy analysis, and project reporting within the BRET consortium.

By submitting this form, you confirm that:

  • You are participating voluntarily
  • You are at least 18 years old
  • You consent to the anonymous use of your responses for research purposes
