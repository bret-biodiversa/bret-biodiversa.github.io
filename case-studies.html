BRET Case Studies

BRET will conduct six case studies across Europe, covering offshore wind, bioenergy, solar parks, onshore wind, hydropower, and biodiversity valuation in Spain. These case studies provide real-world contexts to evaluate biodiversity impacts and develop science-based mitigation and policy strategies.

Funded by Biodiversa+ under 2024–25 BiodivTransform joint Call. Co-Funded by the EC (GA No. 101052342). Coordinated by MGEO, Lund University.
