Overview of Work Packages

The BRET project is structured into six interconnected work packages (WPs), ensuring scientific excellence, policy relevance, and stakeholder engagement across the renewable energy transition.

WP1 — Project Management and Dissemination

Ensures efficient coordination, communication, reporting, and dissemination of project outcomes.

WP2 — Baseline Assessment for Biodiversity-Positive RET

Develops biodiversity baselines and integrates ecological data into renewable energy planning.

WP3 — Framework for Ecological Impact Assessment

Establishes structured ecological impact assessment methodologies based on DPSIR principles.

WP4 — Pathways for Biodiversity Valuation

Combines ecological, economic, and socio-cultural perspectives to assess biodiversity value.

WP5 — Evidence-Based Recommendations via Case Studies

Applies frameworks to real-world case studies and develops actionable policy guidance.

WP6 — Stakeholder Engagement

Engages policymakers, developers, and civil society to co-design biodiversity-positive solutions.

Funded by Biodiversa+ under 2024–25 BiodivTransform joint Call. Co-Funded by the EC (GA No. 101052342). Coordinated by MGEO, Lund University.
